Game Overview

Monster Survivors is a thrilling survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. Use your skills, strategy, and quick reflexes to defeat enemies and become the ultimate survivor. Play directly in your browser without downloads or installations!

How to Play

Use your keyboard or touch controls to move, attack, and dodge. Collect power-ups and upgrades to enhance your abilities. Survive as long as you can and aim for the highest score!
