Game Previews

Bloxorz

Bloxorz is a classic 3D puzzle game that has captivated millions of players since its release in 2007. Your mission is simple yet challenging: guide a rectangular block through intricate mazes and drop it into a square hole at the end of each level. With clever mechanics like switches, fragile tiles, and teleports, Bloxorz offers a perfect blend of strategy, logic, and fun. Are you ready to test your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills?

How to Play Bloxorz

- **Move the Block:** Use the arrow keys (or on-screen buttons for mobile) to tilt and flip the block across the platforms.
- **Avoid Falling:** Ensure your block doesn’t fall off the edge of the platform.
- **Fragile Tiles:** Be cautious of orange tiles—they will crumble if your block stands on them vertically.
- **Activate Switches:** Stand on switches to open or close bridges. X-shaped switches require the block to stand vertically, while round switches can be activated in any position.
- **Use Teleports:** Some tiles split your block into two cubes and teleport one of them to a different location. Use the spacebar (or toggle button) to control each cube individually and reconnect them.
- **Reach the Goal:** Navigate the block to the square hole at the end of each level to progress.

Features of Bloxorz

- **Challenging Puzzles:** Solve 33 increasingly difficult levels that test your logic and planning skills.
- **Unique Mechanics:** Navigate switches, fragile tiles, and teleports to overcome obstacles.
- **Save and Resume:** Save your progress with a password system or rely on automatic browser saves.
- **Timeless Gameplay:** A perfect mix of strategy and fun that keeps players coming back for more.
- **Cross-Platform Play:** Enjoy Bloxorz on both PC and mobile devices.

Controls

- **PC:** Use the arrow keys to move the block. Press the spacebar to toggle between cubes when split.
- **Mobile:** Use the on-screen buttons to move the block and toggle between cubes.

Tips for Success

- Plan your moves carefully—rushing can lead to mistakes.
- Use switches strategically to open paths and avoid dead ends.
- Pay attention to fragile tiles and ensure your block is positioned correctly.
- When using teleports, think ahead to reconnect the split cubes.
- Take note of the password displayed on the screen to resume your progress later.
