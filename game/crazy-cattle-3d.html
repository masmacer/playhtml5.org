Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to Crazy Cattle 3D! This is a thrilling and fast-paced action game where you take control of a wild cattle herd, navigating through challenging terrains and obstacles. Are you ready to experience the ultimate cattle adventure?

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

- Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your cattle.
- Avoid obstacles like fences, rocks, and rivers.
- Collect coins and power-ups to boost your score.
- Complete each level within the time limit to unlock new challenges!
- Stay focused and guide your herd to victory!
