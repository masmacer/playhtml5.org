Squad Shooter

Step into the action-packed world of Squad Shooter, a fast-paced browser-based shooting game inspired by the popular Brawl Stars style. Navigate through dangerous arenas, eliminate enemies, and collect loot to upgrade your character and weapons. With simple controls and auto-targeting mechanics, Squad Shooter lets you focus on strategy and survival in an exciting 3D environment!

How to Play Squad Shooter

- Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character.
- On mobile devices, use the virtual joystick to navigate.
- Your weapon auto-targets and fires when enemies are in range.
- Defeat enemies to collect coins, health packs, and special cards.
- Open treasure chests to unlock powerful rewards.
- Find the exit to complete each level and progress to the next challenge.

Game Features

- Over 40 unique levels with increasing difficulty.
- Stunning 3D graphics and immersive arenas filled with loot.
- Auto-targeting mechanics for seamless gameplay.
- A variety of weapons to unlock and upgrade, including shotguns, lava guns, and Tesla guns.
- Character upgrades to enhance health and survivability.

Weapons in Squad Shooter

- Minigun: Your starting weapon, perfect for rapid-fire attacks.
- Shotgun: Unlock with 5 cards, ideal for close-range combat.
- Lava Gun: Unlock with 10 cards, deals powerful fire damage.
- Tesla Gun: Unlock with 10 cards, zaps enemies with electricity.

Why You'll Love Squad Shooter

- Easy-to-learn controls with engaging gameplay.
- A mix of strategy and action in every level.
- Free to play online with no downloads required.
- Perfect for fans of shooting games and fast-paced action.
