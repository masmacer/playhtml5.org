Masked Special Forces

Step into the action-packed world of Masked Special Forces, a thrilling first-person shooter game where you join an elite team of masked operatives. Engage in intense battles, complete challenging missions, and prove your skills in this fast-paced combat experience!

How to Play Masked Special Forces

- Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move your character.
- Left mouse click to fire your weapon.
- Right mouse click to aim for precise shots.
- Press the spacebar to jump and avoid enemy fire.
- Press "Q" to slide while running.
- Hold Left Shift to sprint and move faster.
- Use the number keys (1, 2, 3) or scroll to switch weapons.
- Press "E" or "F" to interact with objects or pick up weapons.
- Press "G" to throw dynamite if available.
- Press "H" to use medkits and restore health.
- Press "T" to chat with teammates.
- Press "L" to toggle cursor lock for better control.
- Hold "Tab" to view the scoreboard.
- Press "Escape" to pause the game or access the menu.

Why You'll Love Masked Special Forces

- Intense first-person shooter gameplay with realistic graphics.
- A variety of weapons to choose from, including rifles, pistols, and grenades.
- Multiple game modes, including Team Deathmatch and Free-for-All.
- Challenging missions that test your shooting and tactical skills.
- Perfect for fans of FPS and action-packed combat games.
