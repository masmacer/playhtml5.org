Game Previews

Unblock It 3D

Test your logic and spatial skills in Unblock It 3D, a free online puzzle game that challenges your brain with engaging 3D block puzzles. Rotate, slide, and clear blocks to solve intricate puzzles across multiple game modes. With intuitive controls and progressively challenging levels, Unblock It 3D is perfect for puzzle enthusiasts of all ages!

How to Play Unblock It 3D

- **Rotate the View:** Click and drag (or swipe) to rotate the 3D cluster and inspect all six faces.
- **Slide Blocks:** Click (or tap) on a block to slide it in the direction of its arrow.
- **Use Boosters:** Activate power-ups like the Arrow Switch, Bomb, or Hint to overcome tricky puzzles.
- **Classic Mode:** Solve puzzles at your own pace with unlimited moves.
- **Adventure Mode:** Complete levels within a set number of moves to unlock new worlds and challenges.

Features of Unblock It 3D

- **Dynamic Gameplay:** Rotate and slide blocks in a fully interactive 3D environment.
- **Two Game Modes:** Enjoy relaxed gameplay in Classic Mode or test your strategy in Adventure Mode.
- **Challenging Levels:** Solve hundreds of handcrafted puzzles with increasing complexity.
- **Historic Worlds:** Explore themed levels inspired by Ancient Egypt, Medieval Europe, the Aztec Empire, and Feudal Japan.
- **Boosters and Power-Ups:** Use tools like the Arrow Switch, Bomb, and Hint to solve even the toughest puzzles.

Boosters and Power-Ups

- **Arrow Switch:** Rotate a block's arrow to open new paths.
- **Bomb:** Remove a small cluster of blocks instantly.
- **Hint:** Highlight a possible move to guide you through tricky situations.
- Boosters recharge faster in Adventure Mode when you complete levels under the move limit.

Why You'll Love Unblock It 3D

- **Brain-Boosting Fun:** Sharpen your logic and spatial reasoning skills.
- **Relaxing and Engaging:** Enjoy intuitive controls and satisfying puzzle-solving mechanics.
- **Replayability:** With hundreds of puzzles and two game modes, there's always a new challenge waiting.
- **Free to Play:** Play directly in your browser without downloads or subscriptions.

Controls

- **PC:** Click and drag to rotate the view, click to slide blocks.
- **Mobile:** Swipe to rotate the view, tap to slide blocks.
