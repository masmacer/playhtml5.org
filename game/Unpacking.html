Unpacking

Welcome to Unpacking, a relaxing and satisfying home decoration and organization puzzle game. Dive into the cozy world of unpacking boxes, organizing rooms, and creating the perfect living space. With its simple yet engaging gameplay, Unpacking is the ultimate game for those who love tidying up and decorating!

How to Play Unpacking

- **Unpack Furniture:** Start by opening boxes in various rooms, including the kitchen, living room, bedroom, and more.
- **Organize Items:** Drag and drop items to their correct places. Once an item is placed correctly, its position will be locked.
- **Arrange Drawers and Shelves:** Sort smaller items into drawers, closets, and shelves for a perfectly organized space.
- **Earn Stars:** Complete levels quickly to earn stars. Finish within 90 seconds for 3 stars, or take your time and enjoy the process.
- **Challenge Yourself:** Replay levels to improve your score and unlock new areas to organize.

Features of Unpacking

- **Relaxing Gameplay:** Enjoy a stress-free experience as you organize and decorate rooms.
- **Multiple Rooms:** Explore and tidy up 11 unique rooms, including the kitchen, bathroom, kids' room, and even the garden.
- **Challenging Levels:** Test your organizational skills with increasingly complex puzzles.
- **Hints Available:** Stuck on a level? Use hints to see a preview of the completed room.
- **Cozy Atmosphere:** With its warm visuals and calming gameplay, Unpacking is perfect for unwinding.

Tips for Playing Unpacking

- Start with larger furniture pieces before organizing smaller items.
- Think logically about where items belong. For example, pillows go on beds or sofas, and TVs should face seating areas.
- Divide similar items into groups to make organizing easier.
- Use hints if you're stuck, but try to solve puzzles on your own for a greater sense of accomplishment.

Controls

- **PC:** Use your mouse to navigate. Hold the left-click to drag and place items.
- **Mobile:** Tap on items to select them, then drag them to their desired locations.
