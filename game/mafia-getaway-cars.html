Mafia Getaway Cars

Immerse yourself in the thrilling world of Mafia Getaway Cars, an action-packed driving game where you take on the role of a getaway driver for the mafia. Navigate through intense city streets, evade the police, and complete daring missions to prove your skills behind the wheel!

How to Play Mafia Getaway Cars

- Drive through the city to complete missions assigned by the mafia.
- Avoid crashing into obstacles and other vehicles.
- Evade police chases by using your driving skills and quick reflexes.
- Collect power-ups and cash to upgrade your car.
- Complete missions within the time limit to unlock new challenges and vehicles.

Controls

- Arrow Keys / WASD: Steer, accelerate, and brake.

Why You'll Love Mafia Getaway Cars

- High-speed action with intense police chases.
- A variety of missions that test your driving skills.
- Multiple cars to unlock and upgrade for better performance.
- Stunning 3D graphics and immersive city environments.
- Perfect for fans of driving and action games.
